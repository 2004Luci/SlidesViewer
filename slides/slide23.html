Audit Qualification: Real Case & Board Clarification

Company

Jet Airways (India) Ltd. - Listed airline company that faced severe financial distress leading to suspension of operations in 2019.

Annual Report FY 2018-19

Audit Qualification

Auditors expressed concerns regarding the company's ability to continue as a going concern due to heavy losses, loan defaults, and negative net worth.

Qualified / Emphasis of Matter

Directors' Clarification

The Board stated that management was actively pursuing a resolution plan under the Insolvency & Bankruptcy Code (IBC) and expected revival through a strategic investor.

Sec 134(3)(f) Explanation

Governance Lesson

Directors must clearly explain audit qualifications in the Board's Report, outlining causes and corrective plans to maintain transparency with shareholders and regulators.

CS ensures disclosure quality

CS Perspective: Explaining Audit Qualifications

Under Section 134(3)(f) of the Companies Act, 2013, the Board must provide explanations for every qualification, reservation, or adverse remark made by auditors. The Company Secretary plays a crucial role in ensuring the explanation is legally compliant, factually accurate, and demonstrates corrective action to reassure investors and regulators.
