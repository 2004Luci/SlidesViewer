CS Role / Financial Reporting

Governance Responsibilities

Board & Audit Committee Oversight

Board Approval (Sec 134): Ensure financial statements are approved by the Board and signed by Chairperson/Directors, CEO, CFO, and CS.

Audit Committee (Sec 177): Facilitate review of quarterly/annual financial results before Board submission; monitor auditor independence.

AGM Adoption: Ensure timely placing of accounts before shareholders at the Annual General Meeting.

Reporting & Specific Compliance

Board's Report: Verify completeness of Directors' Report under Sec 134(3), including AOC-1 (Subsidiaries) and AOC-2 (RPTs).

Related Party Transactions (Sec 188): Oversee governance policy, Omnibus approvals, and arm's length justification.

CSR Compliance (Sec 135): Monitor spend against obligation, transfer of unspent amounts, and committee constitution.
