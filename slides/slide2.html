Primary Objectives

True & Fair View: Reflecting accurate financial position, performance, and cash flows.

Decision Usefulness: Providing critical data for investment and credit decisions.

Stewardship & Accountability: Management's report card to shareholders regarding resource utilization.

Regulatory Framework & Compliance

Legal Basis: Section 129 of Companies Act, 2013 read with Schedule III.

Reporting Standards: Compliance with Accounting Standards (AS) or Ind AS as applicable.

Statutory Filings: Mandatory filing of Forms AOC-4 / AOC-4 XBRL with ROC.

Approval Process: Board approval and signing under Section 134 before AGM circulation.
