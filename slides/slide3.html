Key Components

Core Statements

Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss, Cash Flow Statement, and Statement of Changes in Equity (for Ind AS companies).

Exemptions (Sec 2(40))

Cash Flow Statement is not mandatory for One Person Companies (OPC), Small Companies, and Dormant Companies.

Critical Linkages

P&L → BS: Net Profit flows to Retained Earnings.

BS ↔ CFS: Cash/Bank balance must tally with closing cash.

P&L → CFS: Profit adjusted for non-cash items = Operating Cash.

Notes to Accounts

Integral part of FS; contains significant accounting policies, critical estimates, and detailed schedules.
