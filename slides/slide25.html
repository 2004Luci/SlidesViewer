Statutory Compliance / Section 148

Cost Records & Cost Audit: Rules 2014

Category
Rule / Requirement
CS Verification & Role
Priority
Applicability Check
Turnover Thresholds (Rule 3)

Regulated Sectors: Overall turnover ≥ ₹35 Cr.
Audit: Overall ≥ ₹50 Cr & Product ≥ ₹25 Cr.

Critical
Record Maintenance
Form CRA-1 (Rule 5)

Ensure cost records are maintained regularly in CRA-1 format. Verify integration with financial accounts.

Mandatory
Auditor Appointment
Form CRA-2 (Rule 6)

File CRA-2 within 30 days of Board meeting or 180 days of FY start. Check Board Resolution.

Critical
Audit Report
Form CRA-3

Receive report from Cost Auditor within 180 days of FY end. Board to review reservations/qualifications.

Mandatory
Filing with ROC
Form CRA-4 (XBRL)

File Audit Report with Central Govt within 30 days of receipt. Attach Board's explanation on qualifications.

Critical
Casual Vacancy
Vacancy Filling

Fill vacancy within 30 days via Board Meeting. File new CRA-2 with ROC.

Procedural
Exemptions
Audit Exemption

Verify if Export Revenue > 75% or operating in SEZ (Rule 3). Document exemption rationale.

Info

Note: Non-compliance with Section 148 attracts penalties for both Company (min ₹25k) and Officers/Auditors. CS must track turnover limits annually.
