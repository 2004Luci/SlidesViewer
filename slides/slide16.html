Governance / Section 134

CS Signing of Financial Statements

Legal Requirement (Section 134)

Financial Statements must be approved by the Board of Directors and signed on behalf of the Board by:

Chairperson: If authorized by the Board.

Directors: Two directors, one of whom shall be the Managing Director (if any).

Chief Executive Officer (CEO): If he is a director.

Chief Financial Officer (CFO): Wherever appointed.

Company Secretary (CS): Wherever appointed (Mandatory for paid-up capital ≥ ₹10 Cr).

Authentication & Circulation

Date & Place: Must be clearly mentioned; precedes the auditor's report date.

Attachments: Board’s Report, Auditor's Report, and Annexures (AOC-1, AOC-2).

Circulation: To members at least 21 days before the AGM.
