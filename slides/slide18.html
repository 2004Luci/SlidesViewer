Types of Audit Opinions

Unmodified (Unqualified)

"Clean Report". Financial statements give a true and fair view in all material respects.

Qualified Opinion

"True and fair, except for..." Misstatements are material but not pervasive. Requires Board explanation.

Adverse Opinion

"Do NOT give a true and fair view." Misstatements are both material AND pervasive. Serious red flag.

Disclaimer of Opinion

Auditor unable to obtain sufficient evidence. Significance is material and pervasive.

CS Action Point

Any modified opinion (Qualified, Adverse, Disclaimer) triggers Section 134(3)(f): The Board's Report must include full explanations or comments by the Board on every qualification, reservation, or adverse remark.
