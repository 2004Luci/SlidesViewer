Impact of Authentication

Statutory Responsibility

The CS is not just a witness but a Key Managerial Personnel (KMP) ensuring the accuracy of statutory disclosures and vetting compliance with the Companies Act, 2013 and SEBI LODR.

Legal Consequences

Incorrect sign-offs can lead to penalties under Section 448 (False Statements) and Section 447 (Fraud), alongside professional misconduct charges by ICSI.

Protective Controls

Mitigate risk through robust documentation: detailed Board Minutes, compliance checklists, working papers, and obtaining Management Representation Letters (MRLs).

Stakeholder Trust

A CS-certified financial statement acts as a governance seal, enhancing transparency for investors, regulators, and lenders, and signaling a culture of ethical compliance.
