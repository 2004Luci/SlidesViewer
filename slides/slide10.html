Analysis / Financial Ratios

Ratio Analysis: What, Why, How

Definition

Mathematical relationships that contextualize absolute financial numbers to assess performance, health, and efficiency. Provides meaning to standalone figures.

Purpose & Utility

  • Comparability: Benchmarking against past years (Trend) or industry peers (Cross-sectional).
  • Covenant Monitoring: Tracking debt limits (e.g., Debt/Equity < 2:1).
  • Statutory: Schedule III mandates disclosure of key ratios & explanation for >25% change.

CS Cautions

Ensure consistency in accounting policies. Adjust for seasonality and one-off items (abnormal gains/losses) before interpretation.
