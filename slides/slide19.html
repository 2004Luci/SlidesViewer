Notes to Accounts: Deep-Dive Disclosures

Crucial Components for Review

Accounting Policies & Estimates: Basis of preparation, revenue recognition, critical judgments, and changes in policies.

Detailed Schedules: PPE movement, Working Capital ageing (receivables/payables), provision analysis, and contingent liabilities.

Specific Disclosures: Related Party Transactions (RPT), CSR spending vs obligation, and ratio analysis variance explanations.

CS Verification & Compliance Trail

RPT Compliance (Sec 188): Verify Note disclosures against Audit Committee minutes and Omnibus approvals.

Loans & Investments (Sec 185/186): Check limits, purpose, and board resolutions matching the Note schedules.

CSR (Sec 135): Cross-verify unspent amounts in Notes with transfer to Unspent CSR Account.

Deposits (Sec 73): Ensure no "deemed deposits" hide under 'Other Current Liabilities'.
