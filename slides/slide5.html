Verification Areas

Statutory Filings

Ensuring timely filing of AOC-4/XBRL (Financials), MGT-7/7A (Annual Return), ADT-1 (Auditor Appt), and charge documents (CHG-1/4).

Liaison & Coordination

Acting as the critical bridge between Management, Statutory Auditors, and Regulators (MCA/SEBI) to resolve queries.

Disclosure Verification

Verifying alignment with Schedule III formats, Ind AS/AS requirements, and ensuring all CARO-related data points are accurate.

Monitoring Limits

Tracking compliance with Section 180 (Borrowing limits), Section 73–76 (Deposits), and Section 197 (Managerial Remuneration caps).
